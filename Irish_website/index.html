Discover Ireland!

Ireland is a fascinating destination full of history, traditions, and unparalleled natural landscapes. From lush fields to medieval castles, and cozy cities, Ireland is waiting for you with open arms!

Travel Tips

Ireland has unpredictable weather, so we recommend bringing comfortable and weather-appropriate clothing. Don’t forget your camera!

Currency

The official currency is the Euro (€). Make sure to carry some cash, although credit cards are widely accepted.

Language

The official language is Irish, but English is the most spoken language.
